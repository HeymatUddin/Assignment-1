Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Harnessing technology, research, and creative solutions to address challenges and unlock new opportunities.

Sustainability

Prioritizing environmental and social well-being for a balanced, long-term future.

Infrastructure

Building world-class physical and digital infrastructure to enable progress.

Empowerment

Enabling individuals and communities to thrive through education, inclusion, and opportunities.

The Vision of Future

A forward-looking Bangladesh driven by innovation, sustainability, and empowerment, where technology, renewable energy, and world-class infrastructure transform lives and create opportunities for all.

Digital Transformation

Pioneering a tech-driven society with seamless connectivity, e-governance, and AI-powered solutions, enabling smarter services, efficient industries, and an empowered digital economy.

Green Energy

A commitment to renewable resources like solar, wind, and hydro, ensuring sustainable growth while combating climate change and protecting Bangladesh's rich natural heritage.

Modern Infrastructure

Developing cutting-edge transport, energy, and urban systems to support a thriving economy, elevate living standards, and create future-ready cities.

The world is changing, and so must we. Together, let us write the next chapter of our nation’s history—a chapter of progress, prosperity, and peace. Let us build a Bangladesh that the world will look up to, and that future generations will be proud to inherit.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Advisor of the Republic of Bangladesh

Tamim Iqbal Retires from International Cricket

Tamim Iqbal, former captain of Bangladesh's one-day cricket team, has announced his retirement from international cricket. Debuting in 2007, Tamim played 243 ODIs, 70 Tests, and 78 T20Is, amassing over 15,000 runs and 25 centuries—the most by any Bangladeshi batter.
